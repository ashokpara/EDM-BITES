# Festival Season 2026: What We're Watching

Summer is almost here, and so is festival season. We've been scouring lineups across the globe to bring you the must-know events of 2026.

## Tomorrowland

Belgium's crown jewel returns with what might be its most ambitious lineup yet. Multiple stages spanning every sub-genre, with a focus on giving rising acts prime-time slots alongside the legends. The mainstage closing set on Saturday night is rumored to feature a very special back-to-back that nobody will see coming.

## EDC Las Vegas

The Electric Daisy Carnival is leaning harder into production values this year. New kinetic stage structures, an upgraded Cosmic Meadow with expanded capacity, and a curated "Classics" tent running golden-era sets all weekend.

## Fabric Weekender, London

For the underground faithful. No main stage, no pyrotechnics — just impeccable sound systems and curation that prioritizes the music above everything else. The techno programming this year is particularly strong.

## What to Watch

- **New headliners** breaking through from the underground
- **B2B sets** that only festival season can produce
- **Stage collisions** between different scenes and sounds

Get your tickets early. These events sell out faster every year.
