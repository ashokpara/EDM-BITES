# Top 5 EDM Drops of 2026 So Far

Four months in and the year is already delivering hard. Here are the five drops that have had floors erupting since January.

## 5. Hypernova — "Event Horizon"

A prog-trance odyssey clocking in at 9 minutes. The build alone takes four minutes, and the payoff is absolutely worth it. Hypernova has been quietly ascending the ranks and this track announces their arrival.

## 4. VLTRA — "Cascade Protocol"

Melodic techno with a dark edge. The layered percussion and eerie synth stabs make this one for the late-night slots. VLTRA keeps pushing the sound forward.

## 3. Solaris & Kira Dawn — "Freefall"

A collab nobody expected but everyone needed. Solaris's signature big-room sound meets Kira Dawn's vocal-led melodic house — the result is euphoric.

## 2. Phantom Circuit — "Zero Kelvin"

Pure acid techno chaos. This track has been destroying warehouses since February and shows no signs of cooling down (pun intended).

## 1. Oracle — "The Reckoning"

Yes, we named the blog after them, and yes, this track earns the top spot. A masterclass in tension and release. The drop at 4:32 might be the best moment in EDM this decade so far.
